A few tricks that make for a way more shareable moment.

✊

Hold a fist to charge

Clench your hand and hold it — power builds up around your fist the longer you hold, with the effect's own colors and style.

💥

Release for the blast

Open your fist to unleash everything you charged up — bigger holds mean a bigger, more dramatic blast on camera.

🤜🤛

Combine both fists

Bring two charged fists together to merge their power into one much bigger combo blast — pull apart to split it again.

✌️

Try hand signs

✌️ peace sign for confetti, 👍 thumbs up for floating hearts, ✋ open palm for a force push, 👉 point to fire a laser — on top of any effect.
